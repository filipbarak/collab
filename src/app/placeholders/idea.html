  • Introduction

    • This is Adel’s I2 template for Idea Incubation. The following sections are offered as guidance to help the Innovator communicate their idea to the community members. Any member can add new sections as appropriate. The purpose of this document is to create a public document that will be posted as a marketing promotion of the idea. This will reside on the Adel homepage (adel.io), as well as other locations utilized by Adel. No confidential or sensitive information will reside in this document. Any sensitive details will be moved to the P2 Project Planning document by the Community Administrators, or the Project Review Committee, once that phase is activated. I2 helps promote Adel’s incubation initiative, and helps bring new members to the community. New members bring more expertise, more passion, and more entrepreneurs, to help incubate blockchain projects that will change the world.

  • Abstract

    • This section should be around four to eight sentences, outlining a short overview of the idea

  • Solution Overview

    • This section expands on the abstract by providing a more detailed description of the idea. An infographic or other visual tool is recommended

  • Market Landscape

    • What are the Unique Selling Points (USPs) of the idea? What are the Target Markets of the idea? What are the Features & Benefits of the idea?

  • Monetization

    • What are the Revenue Opportunities for the idea? Outline the various approaches to how the idea will generate revenue (e.g.. Software licensing, service fees, project fees, training, etc.) What are the Channels to market, or Partnerships needed to ensure success of the idea? What is the Regional focus (e.g. German, EMEA, global, etc.). Does a regional focus require changes to the idea (e.g.. Language translations, different currencies, price levels due to varying market economics, etc.)

  • Development

    • What are the Software & Hardware requirements to launch the idea. This section should include the Implementation, Time, Resources, and Expertise needed to realize this idea.

  • Swot Analysis

    • What are the Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities & Threats of the idea? Any weaknesses or threats raised in this section should follow with mitigating solutions to overcome those challenges.
